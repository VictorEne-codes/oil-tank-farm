Aviation Fuel

Rexel Offshore Fuel Products Aviation Fuel

We offer - Aviation Fuel

Rexel Offshore proudly offers aviation fuel, formerly known as "kerosene," tailored for a wide range of jet aircraft applications. Our aviation fuel complies with rigorous international standards, including the latest versions of AFQRJOS, British DEF STAN 91-91, ASTM D1655, and NATO F-35 specifications.

With a minimum flashpoint of 38°C and a maximum freezing point of -47°C, our aviation fuel ensures optimal performance and safety. JET A-1, the primary fuel for jet turbine engines, is also suitable for general aviation, including compatible diesel engine planes.

In the realm of military aviation, including F16 and fighter jets, Rexel Offshore's aviation fuel stands out for its quality and reliability. As planes soar through the air, our fuel provides the necessary energy to power these high-performance aircraft.

Jet fuel, also known as aviation turbine fuel (ATF), is specifically formulated for gas-turbine engine-powered aircraft. Our colorless to straw-colored fuels, Jet A and Jet A-1, adhere to standardized international specifications, ensuring consistency and excellence.

For civilian turbine-engine aviation, our Jet B fuel is notable for its enhanced cold-weather performance. Jet fuel is a precisely balanced mixture of various hydrocarbons, with kerosene-type jet fuels (such as Jet A and Jet A-1) featuring a carbon number distribution between about 8 and 16.

In contrast, wide-cut or naphtha-type jet fuel (including Jet B) ranges between about 5 and 15 carbon atoms per molecule. At Rexel Offshore, we prioritize quality and performance in every drop of aviation fuel we provide.
